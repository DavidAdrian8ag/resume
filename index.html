My Process for Freelancing

01

Initial Contact

You first contact me over my social networks or by email with your idea and requirements so I can give you feedback about the implications of that job you are requesting.

02

First Meeting

We get in touch remotely (meets, zoom, etc.), I will ask detailed information about anything I am not clear about the project, and also we will discuss the expectations and limits of the project.

03

Proposal

Once I have all the information I need, I will give you a proposal including budget, timetable and resources in case they are needed (like information, photos, etc.).

04

50% deposit

If you agree the proposal, then I will need the 50% payment in advance of the complete project and all the extra information I might need. So I can begin working on your project.




05

Drafts and Acceptance from Customer

Once I am done with the first draft or drafts (like in a logo request), I will reach you to check if you like the work and to tweak if needed, no major changes are allowed at this point.

06

Complete Payment

When the job is done and you are satisfied with the result, you will be asked to fulfill the rest of the payment.

07

Relese material

Once the payment is settled, I will send all the original material done by me, and will delete all the rest of the information you gave me.

08

Support

Some projects will have support and I will give it to you for the time stipulated in the proposal.
